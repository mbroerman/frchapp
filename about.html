Who We Are

FRCH is a multi-disciplinary design firm specializing in Retail Strategy, Interior Design, Graphic Design, and Architecture. Headquartered in Cincinnati, Ohio and with offices in New York City, Chicago and San Francisco, we are one of the largest independently held design firms in the country. We employ over 185 employees and have 46 years of experience and a ward winning recognition for serving the Retail, Food & Beverage, and Hospitality industries.

Why We Do It

We believe in the power of design to drive preference, loyalty, and success for each of our client's and their distinct products, environments, and services. We design to inspire, inform and motivate consumers, we design to generate smart business solutions that are responsive to your goals and objectives. As a firm of creative, strategic and forwards-thinking individuals, we are passionate about differentiating our clients in the marketplace and in the mind of the consumer.
