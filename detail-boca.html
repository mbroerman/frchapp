BOCA

Relocating their five-star restaurant to a landmark building in the heart of a downtown theater and art district, Boca restaurant seized the opportunity to redefine its culinary experience. Once a cozy, new-age neighborhood Italian trattoria, patrons now bask in the twilight sparkle of crystal chandeliers in a two-story dining room designed to make a grand statement at first look. But upon inspection, deep colors, spot lighting and richly textured walls sculpt an intimate persona that perfectly pairs place and cuisine.
