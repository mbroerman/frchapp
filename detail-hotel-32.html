HOTEL 32

In January 2008, an intense fire erupted at The Monte Carlo Resort and Casino destroying a significant portion of the 32nd floor. As the lead architectural designer/program manager for the restoration, FRCH had the 3000-room hotel back up and running in three weeks. Our attention then turned to the redesign of the penthouse suites on floor 32, creating over 30 uniquely different suites, with solutions ranging from two room bays to five room bays in size.
