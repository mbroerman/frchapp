HUSH PUPPIES

To align with the brand’s international premium cache, a refined design language was developed to communicate an elevated lifestyle adaptable across flagship and shop-in-shop locations. Reflective of the brand’s dynamism, a large pivoting frame creates an easily adaptable graphic backdrop for featured merchandise and sets the stage for the social living room within; shifting the service model and encouraging dialogue between guests and associates.. This shifting center floor presentation elevates featured products with custom pieces to highlight Hush Puppies hero products and a kit of parts merchandising system  provides tools to cross merchandise under the umbrella “Hush Puppies selects”. The visual merchandising is refined in material choice and details reflective of the authentic nature of the brand with quartered oak, hand burnished stainless steel and layers of stacked wool felt. Winks and nods reflective of the personal spirit of the brand come to life in subtle visual merchandising gestures throughout the store led ultimately by the hallmark bronze dog statue, the icon of the brand.
