HYATT DENVER

The newly refurbished hotel interior abstractly celebrates the Denver landscape through the use of natural materials. For example, each registration desk pod represents Geodes found in the landscape, cut and left with polished bronze finishes on the desktop and ceiling. Clad in frosted mirror, the central monumental stair captures the Denver sunlight, casting a glowing reflection onto the registration level. The overall design results in a reinvigorated series of warm and inviting public spaces that energize the entire hotel through an artful reflection of the vibrant natural environment that is Denver.
