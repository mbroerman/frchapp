JUDITH LEIBER

For this icon of fashion, the challenge was to reintroduce Judith Leiber to the world as a young fashion brand without alienating their existing core customers. With a new creative director at the helm, the new brand expression was sexy and current, but with an appreciation for the brand’s heritage. As a nod to Judith Leiber’s taste for fine materials and grand statements, a dramatic space is created, punctuated with luxurious materials and furniture. The product categories are layered from fashion lifestyles to heritage collections to create a clear narrative of the evolution of the brand, its heritage and its future.
