JUDITH LEIBER

As the brand’s “Grand Dame of Park Avenue" image had become more of a hurdle than an asset, the challenge was to reintroduce Judith Leiber to the world as a young fashion brand without alienating their existing core customers. With a new creative director at the helm, the new brand expression was sexy and current, but with an appreciation for the brand’s heritage. As a nod to Judith Leiber’s taste for fine materials and grand statements, a dramatic space is created, punctuated with luxurious materials and furniture. The product categories are layered; first a lifestyle presentation to show a mixture of the heritage and the fashion collections, secondly a dramatic shoe salon becomes a central focal point of the store and lastly the destination heritage collection boutique anchors the rear of the space.  This all came together to create a clear narrative of the evolution of the brand, its heritage and it’s future.
