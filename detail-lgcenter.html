LG CENTER

FRCH won an international competition and selected for the development of the master plan and design for LG Center, a 1.1 million square feet urban shopping, entertainment and gateway into a newly developing Korean city. A 400,000 square feet LG Department Store and multiplex cinema anchor the property while shops and restaurants add vibrancy to the plaza-level and adjacent park. A parking garage accommodates guests and rounds out the master plan which provides for an easily accessible, pedestrian friendly and vibrant urban shopping, dining and leisure entertainment destination.
