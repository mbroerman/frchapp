LIVERPOOL
DUTY FREE

Targeted to a young, affluent local market as well as international visitors, Liverpool Duty Free is a unique luxury retail destination designed to resonate with its trend-savvy customers by offering the latest designer brands of cosmetics, fragrances and fashion accessories. The design is modern, simple and bold, paying particular attention to flexibility and scalability. With this new smaller retail venue, Liverpool can now access locations and markets previously too small for a full-line department store.
