LIVERPOOL VERACRUZ

Designed as both a fashion and social hub for a new upscale beach community in Veracruz, Mexico, the Liverpool Department Store is truly a place to unwind, be revived and refreshed. The sun-drenched store celebrates its natural surroundings through a play of light, shade and shadow, materiality and color palate, an artful backdrop to the latest in fashion retail. Anchoring a retail center surrounding an inland marina, this store offers an assortment of fashion, dining, entertainment and service venues making it a lifestyle destination that deeply connects with its community and culture.
