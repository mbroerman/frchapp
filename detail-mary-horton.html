MARY HORTON

Eclectic, retro and feminine, the collections were a direct expression of brand founder, Mary Norton’s personal taste, passions and style. A series of retail spaces were designed to create vignettes of Mary’s life; a Los Angeles garden party, a grand Southern home and a New York City Pied-à-terre. With a residential level of detail, finish and scale, the designs combine interior and exterior spaces into a seamless retail lifestyle experience that is both sophisticated and playful. For a unique brand that is deeply connected to its founder, these retail experiences created a strong bond between her and the brand’s growing customer base.
