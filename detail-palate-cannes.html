PALATE CANNES

Located just across from the famous location of the Cannes Film Festival on the French Cote d'Azur, a new restaurant concept called Palate captures the easy-going lifestyle of this French Riviera beach resort. With two main entrances, an open patio on the beach side captures the city's ocean breezes, bright sunlight and the cool "beach buzz". The town side, shoppers from the bustling sidewalks engage the bar area for a quick bite, glass of Champagne or dessert. The Palate design allows this restaurant to transform from a daytime oasis to a nighttime hot spot for cocktails, tapas, and long conversations.
