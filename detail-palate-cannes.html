PALATE CANNES

Located just across the shoreline Boulevard de la Croisette from the famous location of the Cannes Film Festival on the French Cote d'Azur,  a new restaurant concept called Palate captures the easy-going lifestyle of this French Riviera beach resort. With two main entrances, an open patio on the beach side captures the city's ocean breezes, bright sunlight and the cool buzz of visitors from all over the globe. On the town side, shoppers flow in from the bustling sidewalks to a relaxed bar area for a quick bite, glass of Champagne or dessert. The Palate design is crisp and clean, delivering a friendly elegance and modern European sensibility that allows this restaurant to transform from a daytime oasis to a nighttime hot spot for cocktails, tapas, and long conversations.
