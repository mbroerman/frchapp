SWAY

Partnering with Hyatt Hotels, FRCH worked to re-shuffle the hospitality experience simply by creating new associations between lobby level spaces. Here, we used the design of Sway, a restaurant that combines the flavors and craft of modern southern-style cuisine with the textures, materials and detail of a more urban design vernacular, as the design and space-defining nucleus. Polished wood framing and richly appointed furnishings spill from the restaurant to create a more intimate lobby atmosphere, where southern-styled relaxation is the highest priority. Swayâ€™s bar, boldly positioned to the side of the lobby, easily influences and services the entire lobby creating a truly flexible social hub throughout the day and evening. Locally sourced reclaimed woods and exquisite backlit stained glass combine with handcrafted metal details and well-scaled pieces of rustic artwork to deliver casual vibe of cool elegance all day long.

This lobby was featured in the Fall 2012 issue of Hospitality Style, recognized for its innovation and creative touches. It also recently received a Gold Key Design award at the recent IHMRS Show in New York City.
