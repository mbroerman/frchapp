TIFFANY & CO.

Tiffany & Co. Collections is a new retail concept inspired by the breadth and diversity of Tiffany’s silver jewelry. The store transforms the jewelry shopping experience with curated, open-sell presentations and customizable selections encouraging the shopper to build her accessory wardrobe. Designed as a highly mutable environment, displays are supported by a shifting tableau of custom-designed visual elements. These displays serve as a contextual backdrop to transform the store - weaving themes and changing moods in line with the dynamic sense of the jewelry collections. Tiffany & Co. Collections is an engaging experience through display, details and custom sculpture that is loyal to the brand’s heritage.
