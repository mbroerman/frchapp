TROPICANA

FRCH was selected to design five individual guestroom towers as part of a themed resort complex, connected by a casino and retail concourse at the most prominent corner on the Las Vegas strip. At 12,000,000sf, the Tropicana Mega Resort would have been the largest single building ever built. Its program included 10,224 guestrooms, 105,000sf casino, 250,000sf retail mall, 600,000sf convention center, 3 resort spas, 2 show lounges, 20 restaurant and entertainment venues, parking for 6,500 automobiles and a self contained fire station.
