WHIM

As the jewel of Borgata's loyalty program, WHIM was created as an upscale redemption store for the casino’s loyal patrons. Brashly elegant and playfully outrageous, this retail venue fundamentally changes guest retail options within the gaming industry, focusing not on cash transactions but on point redemption. With concierge service and luxury items from ranging from jewelry and designer accessories, to European linens and home entertainment systems, this new rewards venue delivers the whimsy and thrill of shopping-as-sport while extending the Borgata brand as lifestyle– allowing guests to bring a piece of the Borgata elegance and experience home every time!
